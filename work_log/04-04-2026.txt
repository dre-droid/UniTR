Studied UniTR architecture in more depth: it has a VFE to tokenize lidar points into voxels, 
and a patch embed to tokenize image patches into tokens. It optionally has lidar2image and image2lidar 
projections to fuse the inputs early (we're not using them but we fkin should, otherwise there's not cross attention).

The forward basically has sequential blocks (in unitr.yaml 0: self attention, 1-2 lidar2iamge, 3 image2lidar)

Today we added patch masking to the image branch (since it wasn't implemented in the first run since
it required changing the UniTR class).
